Getting Started

Welcome to the Printify Design Generator! This tool helps you create designs with Google Imagen 4 and add them to your Printify products.

To get started:

  1. Enter your Printify API key in the top right corner
  2. Select a shop from the dropdown after verification
  3. Generate designs or browse your existing products
